Spark (20 min/day)

The Spark Program is a 4-week kickstart to improve your health. Requiring just 20 minutes of your time everyday, this program is designed for busy people who want to get started on your health improvement journey without getting overwhelmed. This program will help set the foundation for long-term health & wellness.

Blaze (60 min/day)

The Blaze Program is an intensive 4-week transformation journey for those who are serious about upgrading their health. It requires a daily investment of 60 minutes, enough experience doing exercises, and a lot of energy. Ideal for committed individuals, Blaze helps you push boundaries, reset your system, and achieve lasting change.

De-Stress Program

The De-Stress Program is a calming 4-week journey to help you reduce stress and restore balance in your life. Using simple yet powerful techniques like breathing, mindfulness, and gentle movement, it helps you feel more in control and at peace. Perfect for anyone feeling overwhelmed, anxious, or just in need of a mental reset.

I-Love-Yoga Program

The I-Love-Yoga Program is a gentle, beginner-friendly series designed to help you build flexibility, strength, and calm through daily yoga practice. Perfect for all levels, it fits easily into your routine and supports both physical and mental wellness. This program helps you connect deeply with your body and mind.

Ignite (40 min/day)

The Ignite Program is an advanced 4-week experience in the 1Step app for those ready to make a serious commitment to their health. It requires 40 minutes a day and combines structured exercise, mindful practices, and focused habit-building. If you're looking to boost fitness, reduce stress, and see visible results — Ignite is your next step.

Weight Loss Program

The Weight Loss Program is a structured 4-week plan designed to help you shed excess fat naturally and sustainably. It combines smart dietary modifications, daily movement, and lifestyle changes that actually work — no crash diets, no extreme workouts. If you’re ready to lose weight the right way and keep it off, this program is for you.

10K-Steps Program

The 10K-Steps Program is a simple, motivating 4-week plan to help you gradually build up to walking 10,000 steps a day. It’s perfect for beginners and busy professionals who want to boost activity without a gym. With daily tips, reminders, and encouragement, this program turns walking into a powerful health habit.

I-Love-Pilates Program

The I-Love-Pilates Program focuses on building core strength, improving posture, and enhancing overall body control through precise Pilates exercises. Designed for all fitness levels, it easily fits into your daily schedule. This program helps increase flexibility, balance, and mind-body awareness for a healthier, stronger you.
